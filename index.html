The Family That Triathlons Together…
By Liz Rodgers

One triathloner in a family is unique. An entire family of triathloners is downright rare. What started with Brian Hoffmaster was adopted by Jill Hoffmaster and trickled all the way to their 10-year-old daughter, Ella. See how the Hoffmasters push each other to go further each and every day.

Alaskan Adventure: Fishing the American River
By Liz Rodgers

Just on the edge of Katmai National Park lays the true fly fisherman’s dream. Isolated salmon fishing at its finest, where the only company — and potential competition — comes in the form of nearby grizzly bears with a shared hunger for some of the finest catches you’ll ever hope to find. Experience the isolated wonder of the American River through this vivid photo essay.

What may seem like an oft-overlooked, interesting pit stop off of the Appalachian Trail carries hundreds of years of history and lore. From its farming roots to its secret past as a WWII prisoner of war camp, this veiled gem is well worth the stop.
